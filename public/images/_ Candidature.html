Ready to go down the rabbit hole?

x

The skills test is in the form of two games. They are designed to test your ability to learn and to become a world-class software engineer.
Don't PANIC! Just be careful, do your best and make sure you complete both games.

  • These games have several levels and are therefore sometimes tiring. Complete as many levels as you can in the time allocated.
  • Don’t be in a rush to play the game today (application will be open for a while)- make sure you are fresh and have at least 2.5 hours and a very good internet connection.
  • You can play the first game (10 minutes), and then choose to start the second game (2 hours) at a later stage. Once you have started a game, you have to finish it.
  • When playing the games, make sure you are in a quiet place, and that your computer has a fast and reliable Internet connection.
  • In the absence of an objective in a game, do not panic: this is normal, you can figure it out for yourself.
